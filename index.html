What is AKPRO?

Academics and Profession (AKPRO) is a division that focuses on fulfilling the needs of its members in the academic realm and professional development. This division also carries out an educational advocacy role, supports the readiness of International Program students in navigating university life through to post-graduation, and serves as a liaison between the educational institution and International Program students.

Double/Joint Degree Information

Before applying to any of the partner universities, students must meet the following minimum criteria:

school Academic Requirement

Passed all courses with a minimum GPA of 3.0 out of 4.0, with the following exceptions:

  • 3.2 – Monash University
  • 3.2 – The University of Sydney
  • 3.25 – University of Duisburg-Essen
  • 3.51 – Industrial Engineering at the University of Queensland

language Language Requirement

An overall IELTS score of 6.5 (with no sub-band less than 6.0) OR a TOEFL iBT overall score of 85 (with no sub-band less than 20).

info Additional Requirement: Students applying to the University of Duisburg-Essen (Germany) must have a B2 Level in German.
